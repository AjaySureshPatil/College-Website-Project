What is autonomous college



An autonomous university typically refers to a university that exercises independent control over its day-to-day operations and curriculum. It is generally associated with universities or institutions and implies that the sponsoring state does not have control over academic matters of the school.

Bachelor of Technology(B.Tech)

Master of Technology

Full form of ME is Master of Engineering and that of MTech is Master of Technology. MTech is a professional postgraduate engineering master degree programme awarded to candidates after completion of two years of study in the discipline of engineering.



It is also spelled as MTech. The duration of this degree program is 2 years depending on different countries and universities.
